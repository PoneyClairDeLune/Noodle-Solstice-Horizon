Cyperus

Curio

Curio is an adorable adventurous bean who was one of the first noodles I ran into. She was learning she has Draconequus blood in her!

The Three of us were able to adventure across the lands and help each other recover things we lost, grow, and find new purpose!

Syren

Syren is a lovely and caring Moff-Draconequus who I met early into my adventures!
Curio found her while trying to buy food supplies for the two of us back when I lost my ability to shape-shift.

She's one of my best friends, and always a blast to be around!
